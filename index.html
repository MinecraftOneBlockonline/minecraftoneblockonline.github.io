📖 About One Block

What Is Minecraft
One Block?

Minecraft One Block is one of the most iconic survival challenges ever created for Minecraft. You spawn floating in an endless void with just a single block beneath your feet — and that one block is all you need.

Every time you mine the block, it regenerates into something new — giving you resources, spawning mobs, and unlocking new phases. From a basic plains biome all the way to the Nether and The End, each phase brings new challenges and rewards.

Our browser version lets you experience this legendary challenge without downloading Minecraft — making it accessible to everyone, on any device, for free.
